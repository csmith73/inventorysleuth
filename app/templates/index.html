Features

Inventory Sleuth is the easiest way to get concrete sales data on the largest E-commerce platforms.

Track Sales

See inventory levels and sales for all sellers of a product on Amazon, Ebay and Walmart online marketplaces. Simply paste the product URL into Inventory Sleuth and we take care of the rest.

Visualize Sales Volume

Inventory Slueth produces graphs of the sales and inventory levels to make it easy to identify trends and pick profitable products. Our algorithm is constantly checking inventory levels of tracked products providing up to date information in your dashboard.

Find Profitable Products

With Inventory Sleuth you can be sure a product is selling well before investing in it. Never get stuck with a slow moving product again!
